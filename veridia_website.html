About Veridia

Veridia is a society built on enduring principles, where vitality, growth, and moral uprightness guide every aspect of life. Named from the Latin "Veriditas," signifying freshness and spiritual vigor, our society represents a new beginning for a generation committed to fostering a just and harmonious community. We believe in the strength of shared values and the collective safeguarding of our people.

Our Pillars of Society

Family Life

The foundational unit of Veridia, where roles are determined by individual strengths. Both parents equally share childcare, household duties, and provision. Extended families form robust support networks, and marriage is a sacred union.

Education System

Structured around vocational training, literacy, and numeracy. Community schools teach core subjects alongside moral and ethical principles. Higher education offers specialized trades, leadership training, and advanced academic studies, with equal access for all genders.

Religious Freedom

Predominantly Christian, Veridia embraces spiritual diversity, protecting freedom of worship for all faiths. Each community has its leaders, and churches serve as hubs for worship, charity, and guidance for their congregants.

Democratic Governance

A representative democracy where citizens elect a "Council of Guardians" and a "High Steward." Laws align with core values and ethical principles. Voting is a civic duty, and all genders are equally eligible for positions based on merit and virtue.

Ethical Economy

A mixed system of regulated capitalism and communal enterprise. Private ownership is encouraged, alongside ethical business practices, fair wages, and responsibility to the less fortunate. Guilds regulate trades, and excessive interest is prohibited.

Our Rich Culture

Meaningful Symbols

  • **The Guardian's Compass:** A stylized star/sun symbol with a compass rose, representing guidance and illumination.
  • **The Oak Tree:** Symbolizes strength, endurance, and wisdom.
  • **The Shepherd's Crook:** Represents leadership, care, and guidance.

Common Veridian Language

Our official language, based on Old English sounds, is simple and direct, fostering clear communication across all aspects of society.

Cherished Traditions

  • **Harvest Blessings:** An annual festival of thanksgiving and feasting.
  • **Coming of Age Pilgrimage:** A journey for youths (16-18) for reflection and spiritual growth.
  • **Community Assemblies:** Regular town hall meetings for civic participation.

Devotional Art

Art is primarily functional and devotional. Stained glass depicts biblical stories, carvings adorn public buildings, and music is choral and uplifting. Literature focuses on moral parables and historical accounts.
